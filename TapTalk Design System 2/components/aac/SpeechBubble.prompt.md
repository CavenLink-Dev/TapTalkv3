Clo's dialogue bubble — a rounded white card with a pointer tail. Pair with <Mascot> for onboarding intros and guidance.

```jsx
<SpeechBubble text="Welcome to TapTalk! I'm Clo." typewriter />
<SpeechBubble tail="top">What would you like me to call you?</SpeechBubble>
```

`tail`: `bottom` (default) or `top`. `typewriter` reveals text char-by-char (`speed` ms/char).
